Sai Teja Erukude

Data Scientist | AI Researcher

Koch Ag & Energy Solutions
M.S. in Computer Science, Kansas State University

Sai is a Data Scientist at Koch Ag & Energy Solutions and a passionate AI researcher. He holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from Kansas State University with a perfect 4.0 GPA. With over five years of experience, Sai specializes in building AI-powered data solutions.

His research explores the intersection of Generative AI, Deep Learning, and practical applications across diverse domains. Sai has authored and published multiple peer-reviewed papers in prominent venues, addressing critical challenges such as bias detection in deep neural networks, generative AI applications in astronomical imaging and explainable AI for text analysis.

Additionally, he serves as a peer reviewer for several reputed journals, contributing to the advancement of the AI research community. His work demonstrates a unique ability to bridge theoretical research with real-world data solutions, making him a valuable contributor to both academic and industry domains.

Research Interests: Generative AI, Deep Learning, Astronomical imaging, Neural Network Bias Detection, Explainable AI, Natural Language Processing
